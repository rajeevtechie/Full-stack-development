DSA

DSA has multiple meanings, most commonly referring to Data Structures and Algorithms, a fundamental concept in computer science and programming. It can also refer to the Digital Signature Algorithm, a standard for digital signatures, or the Direct Selling Association, a trade association for companies that use direct sales. Additionally, DSA can stand for the Digital Services Act, a set of European Union regulations for online platforms.

AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. It encompasses a range of technologies that enable systems to perceive their environment, learn from data, solve problems, and act autonomously to achieve specific goals. AI is a broad field with applications spanning various industries, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and customer service.
